Ambrosia

100ml

Ambrosia embodies the effortless elegance of nature, blending fruity freshness and delicate florals to create a scent that is both uplifting and refined. Inspired by the purity of natural ingredients, this fragrance opens with a burst of bright citrus, evoking the crispness of freshly picked fruit under the sun. The heart unveils a sophisticated floral bouquet, where lush jasmine, soft peony, and gentle lily of the valley intertwine, creating a vibrant and feminine core. The base notes settle into a warm, grounding finish with subtle hints of amber and sandalwood, enhancing the natural sweetness with a touch of depth and warmth. Whether worn for a casual day out or an elegant evening event, Générique offers a refreshing and timeless scent that feels both luxurious and accessible. Housed in a beautifully crafted amber-colored glass bottle, this perfume is designed to protect the purity of the fragrance while providing a stunning visual display. With its mid-range pricing, Générique invites you to indulge in a luxury fragrance that brings nature's finest notes into your everyday life, without the luxury price tag. Available in 50ml and 100ml sizes, Ambrosia is perfect for both personal use and a an elegant gift for someone special.
