Founded in the South of England by Jane Doe, a former botanist with a deep love for wild places and forgotten flowers, Générique was born from a quiet rebellion against the overcomplicated and overbranded. Tired of synthetic scents and sky-high price tags, Jane sought to create something different — a fragrance house rooted in simplicity, sincerity, and sustainability. Each Générique scent is handcrafted in small batches, using ethically sourced essential oils and natural isolates. Générique is committed to transparency, minimal waste, and producing perfumes that feel as good to wear as they do to stand behind.
