About

Ambrosia embodies the effortless elegance of nature, blending fruity freshness and delicate florals to create a scent that is both uplifting and refined. Inspired by the purity of natural ingredients, this fragrance opens with a burst of bright citrus, evoking the crispness of freshly picked fruit under the sun.

Housed in a beautifully crafted amber-colored glass bottle, this perfume is designed to protect the purity of the fragrance while providing a stunning visual display. Générique invites you to indulge in a luxury fragrance that brings nature's finest notes into your everyday life, without the luxury price tag.

Available in 50ml and 100ml sizes, Ambrosia is perfect for both personal use and a an elegant gift for someone special.

The heart unveils a sophisticated floral bouquet, where lush jasmine, soft peony, and gentle lily of the valley intertwine, creating a vibrant and effortlessly feminine core. The base notes settle into a warm, grounding finish with subtle hints of amber and sandalwood, enhancing the natural sweetness with a touch of depth and warmth.

Terms of Services

Founded in the South of England by Jane Doe, a former botanist with a deep love for wild places and forgotten flowers, Générique was born from a quiet rebellion against the overcomplicated and overbranded.

Tired of synthetic scents and sky-high price tags, Jane sought to create something different — a fragrance house rooted in simplicity, sincerity, and sustainability. Each Générique scent is handcrafted in small batches, using ethically sourced essential oils and natural isolates.

Générique is committed to transparency, minimal waste, and producing perfumes that feel as good to wear as they do to stand behind. Read more about our ethics policy here.
